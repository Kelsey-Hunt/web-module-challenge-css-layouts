My Web Development Journey

An Eye for Aesthetic...

Since childhood, I have chased fulfillment through the unique feeling of accomplishment and joy that only comes through creating something new with my own two hands. Throughout my life, I have nurtured my love for design through a wide-variety of art forms including starting my own stained glass studio, designing and refinishing furniture, and adorning my wardrobe with custom embroidery.


Leveraging Technology to Optimize Life...

My strategy for tackling life's challenges has always been "Work hard now so you can relax later." Throughout my career (and at home) I am constantly seeking ways to streamline the process for any task I don't enjoy doing. My interest in web development grew from my appreciation of a well-designed spreadsheet and the efficiency that can be achieved by spending the time to create one. However, after creating a spreadsheet for each of my most dreaded tasks, I have come to terms with the fact that there is only so much that can be achieved through Excel.


Launching a New Career...

I enrolled at BloomTech in 2022 to pursue a career that supports the interests I have developed personally and professionally. Upon graduation, I look forward to designing aesthetically pleasing platforms backed with limitless optimization tools.
